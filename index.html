Why You Should Sign up for Movie Seeker

Reason 1

Movie Seeker saves you time and energy by eliminating the need to search through hundreds of titles and summaries. Let Movie Seeker do the searching for you! Input the characteristics of exactly the type of movie you want to watch and Movie Seeker will present you with the best match in a flash.

Reason 2

Do you have specific taste in movies? Are you ever in the mood for something that doesn’t fit cleanly in a generic movie genre? Make customized genres and lists to help Movie Seeker find better matches to suit your preferences!

Reason 3

It can be a pain to search for a specific title across multiple streaming platforms. You can sometimes look between a few and don’t even find the movie you want. With Movie Seeker that information can be found all in one place as it compares what movies are available where. No more switching and searching!

Reason 4

If you have ever tried to watch a movie with a group, you know how hard it is to pick a movie that will please everyone. Movie Seeker can solve that problem! Just adjust your criteria to include a little of what everyone likes and get suggestions that will please your whole party!
